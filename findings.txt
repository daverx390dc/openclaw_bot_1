1. MOMENTUM SCALPING (1-5 min timeframes)
================================================================================
Strategy: Entry on initial breakout momentum, exit on first profit target (0.5-2% gain)
Ideal For: High-volatility altcoins (volatile = faster profits)

Key Parameters:
  - Entry: RSI > 70 (overbought) + Volume surge (>150% MA) + Price > BB Upper
  - Exit: Take profit at +0.5% (tight scalp) to +2% (wider scalp)
  - Stop Loss: 0.3% below entry (tight)
  - Timeframe: 1m, 3m, 5m candles
  - Leverage: 3-5x (high leverage = high PnL but tight SL)

Expected Returns: 0.5-2% per trade x 20-50 trades/day = 10-100% daily if 80%+ win rate
Risk: Requires tight execution, slippage eats profits

Pro Tip: Works best during high-volume hours (US market open, Asia close)


================================================================================
2. MEAN REVERSION SCALPING (3-15 min)
================================================================================
Strategy: Fade extreme moves - buy oversold, sell overbought within same 5-min bar

Key Parameters:
  - Entry: RSI < 30 (oversold) or RSI > 70 (overbought) 
  - Reversal Confirmation: 1-bar pullback, BB lower band bounce, Support hold
  - Exit: Return to RSI 50 (midline) or +0.3-1% profit
  - Stop Loss: Just outside recent swing low/high (0.4-0.8%)
  - Leverage: 5-10x (mean reversion can move fast)

Expected Returns: 0.3-1% per trade x 10-20 trades/day = 3-20% daily
Risk: Requires precise entry timing, false signals = immediate losses

Example Setup:
  - Price touches bottom BB band = instant 80% mean reversion within 3 mins
  - Enter long, target return to middle BB
  - Exit on BB middle breach (avg +0.7% profit)


================================================================================
3. GRID SCALPING (Algorithmic, all timeframes)
================================================================================
Strategy: Place grid of buy/sell orders, profit on each fill (no prediction needed)

Key Parameters:
  - Grid Spacing: 0.3-0.8% between orders (tighter = more trades, lower profit each)
  - Position Size: Divide capital into 20-50 micro-positions
  - Range: Define support/resistance (use 50-day Bollinger Bands)
  - Leverage: 2-3x (grid is leverage-light, volatility does the work)
  - Profit Per Tier: 0.2-0.5% per order fill

Expected Returns: 0.2% x 50 orders filled/day = 10% daily (if range-bound)
Risk: Sideways market = good, breakout = liquidation

Famous Implementation: 3Commas, Kucoin Bot, Grid trading functions
Why it works: Removes emotion, systematic profit taking

4. PUMP & DUMP DETECTION SCALPING (High Risk, High Reward)
================================================================================
Strategy: Early detection of pump signals, front-run dumps, exit before exit liquidity

Key Parameters:
  - Volume Spike Detector: Volume > 300% of 24h MA = pump signal
  - Entry: +5-15% above 1-hour low on volume (catch early pump wave)
  - Exit: Sell into strength at resistance or -2% SL
  - Speed: Execute within 30 seconds of signal detection
  - Leverage: 10-20x (risky but high ROI potential)

Expected Returns: 2-10% per successful pump catch (rare, 10-20% accuracy)
Risk: EXTREME - Pump & dumps are partially predictable but fail often
Warning: Front-running is illegal on traditional markets; varies for crypto

5. MACHINE LEARNING SCALPING (Modern, Complex)
================================================================================
Strategy: Train neural network on 500+ price/volume features, predict 3-min candle

Key Parameters:
  - Models: LSTM, XGBoost, or ensemble (multiple models voting)
  - Features: RSI, MACD, Volume, Volatility, Micro-structure (order flow), Sentiment
  - Labels: +0.5% return = BUY signal, -0.5% = SELL signal
  - Leverage: 5-10x
  - Accuracy Target: 55-60% (seemingly low, but 55% edge compounded = huge returns)

Expected Returns: 1-5% daily (if model is well-tuned)
Risk: Model overfitting, data snooping bias, requires GPU/infrastructure
Complexity: High (requires Python ML skills)

6. VOLATILITY SURFACE SCALPING (Advanced)
================================================================================
Strategy: Trade inverse to short-term implied volatility (vega scalping analog)

Key Parameters:
  - Entry: When volatility drops below 20-day MA (mean revert vol)
  - Exit: Volatility returns to mean or +1% profit (fast move)
  - Timeframe: 5m-15m
  - Leverage: 3-5x
  - Hedging: Offset with opposite position if vol explodes

Expected Returns: 0.5-1.5% per trade x 15 trades/day = 7.5-22.5% daily
Risk: Volatility can spike unpredictably (liquidation risk)

  - Leverage: 1-2x (spread scalping doesn't need leverage)
